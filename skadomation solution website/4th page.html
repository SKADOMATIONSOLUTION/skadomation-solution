We Serve

At Skadomation Solution, we provide end-to-end industrial automation services designed to make your processes smarter, faster, and more reliable. Our core expertise includes PLC programming, where we develop custom logic for machines and systems using industry-leading platforms like Siemens, Allen-Bradley, and Schneider. We design intuitive HMI (Human-Machine Interface) screens that give operators real-time control and visibility over their processes, complete with alarms, trends, and diagnostics. Our SCADA solutions offer centralized monitoring, remote access, and detailed data logging — ideal for plants and facilities that need full operational oversight. Beyond individual components, we specialize in system integration, connecting PLCs, HMIs, SCADA, sensors, drives, and even ERP/MES systems to work as one seamless, intelligent unit. We also offer on-site commissioning, field support, and operator training, ensuring smooth project handover and long-term performance. Whether you're upgrading old systems or building something from the ground up, Skadomation delivers tailored automation solutions backed by expert consultation and reliable support.


PLC Programming

Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) are the backbone of industrial automation. We design and program custom control logic to manage machinery such as conveyors, pumps, and full production lines. Using Siemens, Allen-Bradley, and Schneider platforms, our solutions deliver high reliability, scalability, and uptime tailored to your unique requirements.

SCADA Systems

Our SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems provide centralized, real-time monitoring and control of your entire operation. Features include secure remote access, detailed data logging, alarms, and trend analysis — all designed to optimize efficiency and improve operational decisions.

HMI Design

We create user-friendly and intuitive HMI (Human-Machine Interface) screens that empower operators. Our designs feature real-time visualization, alarms, diagnostics, and trends to ensure smooth operations and fast troubleshooting on the shop floor.

Integration

Our integration service connect PLCs, HMIs, SCADA, sensors, drives, and ERP/MES systems into one seamless, intelligent system. We provide end-to-end solutions including on-site commissioning, field support, and operator training — ensuring smooth handover and lasting performance.
