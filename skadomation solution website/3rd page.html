Company profile

Skadomation solutions headquartered in Salem , India, is a dynamic and growing engineering services startup with a strong focus on industrial automation and control systems. We specialize in the design, development, programming, and commissioning of cutting-edge automation solutions tailored to meet diverse industry needs. With deep technical expertise, our team delivers end-to-end engineering services for automation systems, including AC Variable Speed Drives from design and selection to setup, site commissioning, and seamless integration with control architectures.

Our mission is to deliver reliable, cost-effective solutions and comprehensive technical support to help our clients maximize the value of their investments in modern automation technologies.

We bring proven experience working with Rockwell Automation platforms and software, along with Schneider Electric PLCs and Citect SCADA systems, enabling us to craft robust, scalable, and efficient control solutions.
